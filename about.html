About Crafter


Crafter is a business of handicrafts from Bali that sells various kinds of handicraft products located in Sukawati, Gianyar. This business was founded in 2003 by Mr. Putu Darmawan with 3 of his friends.


At first, our products were not widely known by local and foreign tourists. But, a few years later, our products began to be well-known by local and foreign tourists visiting Bali and they were attracted by the products we made. To make it easier for customers to buy our products, we create a website called Crafter which is expected to make it easier for customers who want to buy our products without having to come to our store.
